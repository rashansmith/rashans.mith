Hi! I'm Rashan and for the Summer 2015 I am a TA with Girls Who Code! During week 4 we teach Intro to Web Development and building a homepage is one of the assignments, so I decided to use this assignment as a way to document my Girls Who Code teaching/learning experience as well!

Week 1

In week 1, we taught the girls intro to Programming with Scratch.

Week 2

In week 2, we taught Python through the Myro Scribbler Robots

Week 3

In week 3, we taught graphics and animation, through Processing JS and Javascript.

Week 4

In week 4, we taught AWeb development, with intros to HTML, CSS and JQuery,and Javascript, as well as API's with Mapbox.js .

Week 5

In week 5, we explored algorithms, data structures cryptography.

Week 6

Week 6 & 7 are final project weeks, where the girls used what they learnt to create something. I am doing one myself :)

Week 7

Finalization of projects to present at Graduation!
